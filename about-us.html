About Us

Our Team

Rita Seara

Immigration Consultant

My journey as an immigration lawyer began in 2016, working for a relocation company in Portugal.

In 2019, I decided to embark on the adventure of opening my own business, doing what I love and using my knowledge in immigration and relocation law to benefit my clients.

One of my greatest assets is my commitment to my clients, partners, and colleagues. Reliability and trust are paramount in this line of work.

To date, I have worked with and supported over one hundred clients in becoming citizens of Portugal. This includes all aspects of visa applications, real estate, taxes, and helping set up a new life in Portugal by finding the right place to live, suitable schools, and more.

While the laws are straightforward, each client's case is unique. My dedication to helping and supporting each individual is my highest priority.

With clients moving to Portugal from all around the world, I am happy and eager to make each one feel at home in this beautiful country from the start.

  • Master's Degree, Civil and Commercial Law | Universidade Católica Portuguesa
  • Law Degree | Universidade Católica Portuguesa
  • Member of the Portuguese Bar Association
